## Notes

- Implementation uses the **ICU `islamic-umalqura` calendar built into Node 22 and modern browsers** — zero dependencies. `gregorianFromHijri` works by approximation + correction loop against `hijriParts` (Intl formats but cannot parse).
- **Finding during testing:** the first test wrongly assumed Ramadan 1446 had 30 days; the utility correctly threw for the nonexistent 1446-09-30 (it was a 29-day month — Eid on 2025-03-30). The test now encodes the true month length and asserts day 30 is rejected — evidence the utility refuses impossible Hijri dates rather than silently resolving them.
- Storage rule holds: all returned dates are Gregorian UTC (noon-anchored for date-only safety); Hijri exists only at the render/input boundary.
