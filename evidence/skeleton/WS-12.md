## Findings that change WS-13 (also in ADR-001 and the spike README)

1. **NULLIF is mandatory in every policy** — pooled reuse leaves the GUC as `''`, and a bare `::uuid` cast throws instead of returning zero rows. Found by scenarios 5/8 failing on first run.
2. **`transactionOptions.maxWait` sizing** — default 2s times out when burst depth exceeds pool size (found by scenario 7 on pool=2).
3. **No external pooler at launch** — the Prisma 7 driver adapter owns an in-process `pg.Pool`; the spike doc's pgBouncer questions are moot until multi-instance scale.
